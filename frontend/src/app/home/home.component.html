Why Plazm?

We curate our social media feeds based on our identities and interests. Since these donâ€™t change very often, all the information that flows from this model remains relevant everywhere all the time. This is great for almost all our information needs.

But there is an exception.

Local and event-driven information represents a fundamental mismatch for this legacy model of social media. Unlike information based on our identity and interests, this kind of content also requires us to be physically nearby to be relevant and meaningful. Legacy social media, with its chronological feeds and social graphs, cannot solve this problem.

Plazm can.

By bringing together both local businesses and local knowledge experts, Plazm focuses on connecting mobile users to their environments in the most direct and intuitive way.

The result is not only a community to share local knowledge, but a living, real-time map that helps us navigate the real world.
